History

Cinderella Corpse was a Ukrainian rock/metal band founded in the 1990s. The band experimented with different styles throughout its career, known for its dark, emotional themes and powerful performances. The group disbanded after the disappearance of its leader and virtuoso bassist, Aleksey Kravchuk, a highly creative personality who also acted in films and vanished around 2010–2015.

Band Members

  • Aleksey Kravchuk – Bass, Founder (disappeared c. 2010–2015)
  • Ivan Dymov – Drums
  • Sergiy Bondarenko – Drums
  • Dmytro Kondrashkov – Guitar
  • Viktor Chebanenko – Guitar (final lineup)
  • Oleksandr Omezhenko – Drums
  • Oleksandr Panchev – Guitar, Backing Vocals, Producer

Music

The band's tracks such as "Смерть всем живым" can be found on YouTube and Music.Youtube.com. The sound mixes heavy guitar, dark industrial influences, and Ukrainian underground rock roots.
